Privacy Policy

Pune Merchants Co-operative Bank Ltd (PMCBL) values the privacy, confidentiality, and security of its customers’ personal information. We are committed to protecting your data and using it solely for lawful purposes and to provide better services.

Applicability

This policy applies to all personal information collected directly or through the bank’s portals, electronic communications, and server interactions.

Types of Information Collected

  • Personal Information: Name, address, contact details, occupation, etc.
  • Sensitive Personal Data: Passwords, financial info, Aadhaar, PAN, biometric data, etc.
  • Non-Personal Information: IP address, browser details, operating system, cookies, etc.

Consent & Choice

Consent is obtained when collecting personal data, and customers are informed of the use and choices regarding their data.

Accuracy

The bank ensures personal information is accurate and current. Customers may request corrections if needed.

Purpose and Usage

  • Processing applications and transactions
  • Maintaining internal records
  • Providing services and customer support
  • Complying with legal regulations
  • Personalizing online experiences

Disclosure / Sharing

Data is shared only under the following conditions:

  • To complete transactions
  • With authorized outsourced entities
  • With customer consent
  • To comply with legal requirements
  • With government agencies or under legal orders

Security

We maintain physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards. Employees are trained in proper data handling. Partners are required to maintain confidentiality.

Retention

Information may be retained for 90 days or as required to fulfill the notified purpose and regulatory compliance.
